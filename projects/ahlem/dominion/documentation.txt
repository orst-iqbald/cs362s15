Smithy card
The smithy card is a kingdom card that is specifically an action card. It increases your handsize by 3 when played. The cost of purchasing this 
card is 4 coins.

Adventurer card
The adventurer card is a kingdom card that is also an action card. It allows you to search through your deck until you receive 2 treasure cards 
to be placed into your hand. The other cards are discarded. This card costs 6 coins.

discardCard() function
The function will remove cards from a player as they are played in a round. First, the card is checked for whether it has a trashFlag to 
determine if it is placed into the discard pile. Then, number of cards available to play during this turn is decremented. Then the card is 
located from within the players hand and is removed. The number of cards in your hand is reduced.

updateCoins() function
The function updates the current total of coins available to a player when they play their treasure cards. First, the total coin count is reset.  
Then the player’s hand is checked for any copper, silver, or gold coin cards and the value of these is added to the coin count for the current 
player’s game state. Lastly, bonus coins are included from other cards that may provide coins.
