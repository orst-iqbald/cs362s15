Smithy:

Playing the smithy gives the player an extra card.
Playing the smithy allows a player to exceed the maximum hand size.



Council Room:

Playing the council room gives the player an extra card.
Playing the council room allows a player to exceed the maximum hand size.



Village:

Playing the village allows a player to exceed the maximum hand size.



Adventurer:

Gold cards aren't added to the hand.
When there are no coins in the deck, the player loses a card.
